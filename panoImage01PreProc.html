Pre-Procedure

Required Instruments:

  • Order from attending for panoramic radiograph
  • Sirona Orthophos XG plus panoramic machine
  • Leaded apron
  • Plastic infection control barrier

Preperations

  • Prepare the patient
    • Remove jewelry (anything made of metal) from the neck up
    • Remove prosthetic removable appliances containing metal
    • Place leaded apron on patient
  • Plastic infection control barrier should be placed over the bite stick
  • Prior to positioning the patient you should:
    • Open Axium
    • Select your patient
    • Open Mipacs by selecting the x-ray icon
    • Alert the panoramic machine that an image will be acquired by selecting the red “P” icon within Mipacs
      • Ensure that the appropriate template is selected (panoramic)
