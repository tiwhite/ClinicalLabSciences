Diagnostic

Indications:

  • Use single crowns in any postion of the mouth including the posterior
  • Use the procera  crown Alumina (0,4mm) for cases in the anterior region. It is indicated for incisor, canine, and premolars teeth, and gives increased porcelain space and even greater esthetic possibilities
  • Molar teeth should only receive the regular procera crown alumina
  • For extra strength Procera crown in zirconia should be used
  • For situations where you need to build up the coping for extra porcelain support, you should use the Procera crown CAD or wax-up techniques

Contraindications:

  • Not recommended when bruxism and/or periodontal problems exist
