Procedure:

  • Select the correct size polycarbonate crown from the kit.
  • Adjust the crown gingivally and proximally with a low speed diamond or carbide bur.
  • Protect the dentin and adjacent soft tissue with a layer of petroleum jelly.
  • Fill the crown shell with a mixture of acrylic resin. Wait until the surface monomer dissipates (surface sheen disappears), and then carefully seat it on the preparation. As a guide to  proper seating, note that the incisal edge or occlusal surface relates correctly to the adjacent teeth.
  • When the reline acrylic resin achieves the rubbery stage, trim excess away from the margin with a No. Â½ Hollenback carver. While the acrylic is seating, remove and replace the temporary crown.
  • Remove the unit when the reline material has set.
  • Trim and smooth.
  • Reline or re-marginate if necessary.
  • Finish and polish the restoration.
  • Cement with temporary cement.
