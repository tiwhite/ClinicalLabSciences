Diagnostic

Indications:

  • Verify and adjust the final fit of the intaglio surface of complete dentures
  • Verify the proper vertical and horizontal relationship of the maxillary and mandibular dentures
  • Perform the final occlusal equilibration of the completed prostheses

Contraindications:

  • Patient did not remove previous prostheses 24hrs prior to the insertion appointment to allow tissue recovery for accurate adjustment of the new prostheses
  • Patient cannot return in 24-72 hours for post insertion evaluation
