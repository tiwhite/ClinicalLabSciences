Diagnostic

Indications:

  • To verify the final esthetic results of the planning procedures in complete denture fabrication
  • Allow the patient and their “significant other” to approve the final esthetic results
  • To verify the functional aspects (phonetic, vertical dimension, anterior-posterior mounted position) of the complete denture set-up

Contraindications:

  • Inability to achieve comfort, stability and retention of the wax-up dentures
